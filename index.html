Client

TuksRes Women In Leadership Academy

The University of Pretoria TuksRes Women in Leadership Academy (TRWLA) was started in 2014 in order to provide a platform through which to enrich the lives of young women at university.

Starting at the beginning of their university career, we focus on providing young female students with opportunities for growth and empowering them to use their potential.

The academy mainly functioned on a paper-based basis whereby all information of the academy was stored from paper to spreadsheet, providing inconsistencies in information.

Our System

Events Management

The functionality to create various types of events that the academy usually hosts such as Functions, Lectures and Community Engagement events.

Content Management

The functionality to upload and manage the specific content needed for Lecture and Community Events.

User Management

The functionality to add academy members to the system and manage their details and access rights effectively.

Reporting

Functionality to create reports on various aspects of the system in order to make future business decisions.
